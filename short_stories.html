Short Stories

How long is a short story? One might as well ask, how long is a piece of string? Edgar Allan Poe said a short story is short enough to read in one sitting. H.G. Wells suggested one should be able to read it aloud in fifteen to fifty minutes. The SFWA defines a short story as having a length of fewer than 7,500 words. Other publishers have their own definitions.
