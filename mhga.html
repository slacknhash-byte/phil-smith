MHGA

Dear PASTOR ELMER DOLLAR,

Thank you for your quick response to our letters, and your prayers for the President’s speedy recovery and good health. I have passed your THOUGHTS and your PRAYERS on to the President and he wishes me to convey to you his personal gratitude for your assistance and continued good works. I hope CHRISTY and your children ELMER JR, BILLY ELMER, and CHRISTY ELMER are all well and happy. Naturally, both the President and    are doing just fine now and hope to meet you soon!

While obviously the President has the physique of a man half his age, and is an accomplished athlete and sportsman, he remains concerned that his recent illness may leave him in a state incapable of discharging his duties of state, or, worse, leaving his efforts to make his beloved country great again only half-done. There was even talk (and I say this to you in the strictest confidence) that he may be entering a situation in which a life-cessation scenario may be occasioned, either by an illness too great for the President’s mighty constitution, or by enemies of the country who wish to see this Great Nation fail.

A further spiritual meeting, called following the opening of hostilities with COUNTRY sharpened these worries, despite the Secretary of War urging him that his efforts were a crusade against the unrighteous. But then who would have thought that he would have come up with a solution? He had done some reading on the subject, and apparently Kings in the Crusades funded the building of monasteries, whose tenants would pray for the soul of the King to ease his transition. Now, obviously we don't have Kings and we're not asking people to take vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience; but a similar principle could be put to work here. More than ever, the President needs you, ELMER, and your faithful to pray for his soul, and even to go further, as I'll outline below.

The President has asked me to approach you in your capacity of SENIOR PASTOR of the ELMER DOLLAR WORD OF CHRIST PROSPERITY ASSEMBLY MINISTRIES to ensure an optimal result in the event of an afterlife entry-and-assignment scenario. We are of course certain that, having the greatest soul of any President before him, greater possibly even than all of them combined, his position at God’s right hand is assured, but further effort from this end of the mortal coil is always advisable. To that end we urge you to remember the fruitful relationship your church has enjoyed in partnership with this Administration.

We understand that your church enjoys tithes from some NUMBER congregants, which amounts to a like number of souls saved. We ask that in the event of a Presidential irretrievable-organ-failure episode, you agree to pledge the salvation of a tenth or ‘tithe’ of these souls. I can see two ways of making this work:

Perhaps this event could be turned to your church’s advantage if those forfeiting their short-term guarantee of salvation are taken from the bottom 10% of your church’s tithers, encouraging greater generosity on the part of your flock. Such an uptick in donations should easily cover the cost of the 10-BERTH GARAGE the Lord requires for you to house the LAMBORGHINI and the OTHER LAMBORGHINI with which he blessed you this past six months.

Alternatively, the President’s position in Heaven having been secured, I am certain that those members of the congregation of ELMER DOLLAR WORD OF CHRIST PROSPERITY ASSEMBLY MINISTRIES who were selfless enough to sacrifice their salvation for him shall be mentioned to God and thus be saved, as surely as the President shall be saved. Even if this benefit takes a while to materialize, we could arrange for a suitable memorial of their names while this administrative delay in Celestial bureaucracy works itself out.

This level of salvation, combined with the salvation pledged by other churches that have enjoyed a blessed and fulfilling partnership with this Administration, should ensure that the President is saved to a degree that befits a man of his status, devotion and holiness. We are sure that, in the unlikely event of his demise, with your help the President will be truly placed to Make Heaven Great Again. He already has many ingenious ideas for reform to the afterlife to make it more palatable to those who have gone to considerable trouble to serve God, including the relocation of less palatable residents of Heaven to areas more suitable to their kind, so as to ensure more deserving entrants have more space in which to spend their afterlife, with walls of smokeless fire to keep out undesirables such as DENOMINATION and NATIONALITY.
