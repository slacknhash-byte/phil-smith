Some Jesus

You journalists always stick your noses in where the don’t belong, spread fake news everywhere. Especially foreign journalists: they’re the worst. This is our country and we do things our way, buddy. If you don’t like it, stay in your own damn country. You need you some Jesus.

So we’ve got some riots. You always get your malcontents, your haters, your drama queens; and you’ve got to take a firm line with them. We’ve invested in the response. Nothing unsightly. Just a few billion. Not on tanks and soldiers, either. Soldiers get qualms. Bunch of sissies, ever since they eighty-sixed ‘Don’t Ask Don’t Tell’. They need them some Jesus.

See, we’ve got a mandate. We have to protect our children from forces that have a vested interest in corrupting them. They invaded us. They’re making the kids talk like them, act like them, dress like them. And they’re not hiding it; they’re proud of it! They’re on our TVs! In our bars! In our libraries reading smut to children! It’s disgusting. The churches are with us on this one in a big way. It’s time to get them and their agenda off our streets so fast their sparkly Jimmy Choos don’t touch the sidewalk. You best believe they need them some Jesus.

Those woke traitors defending them are part of the problem too. They won’t be satisfied until every last masculine trait of our great country is sissified. Well, that changes now, and no perverts and foreign-funded agitators are going to stop it. If they want trouble, if they need them some Jesus, we got that covered.

You might recognise this big guy here. Used to be owned by that Crypto guy, who called it Titanomech-999 but we’re rebranding. The beard, the hair, and sandals are new. I thought he looked kinda like a hippy, but my XO, he's a fan of ZZ Top, says he just looks like Dusty Hill. I can buy that. Still, it blows about pretty good in the wind up there. Remember when Oral Roberts had that vision of a 900’ Jesus? We’re kinda going with that. Only with an M61 Vulcan cannon up each sleeve, and a flamethrower and a water cannon in the sacred heart.

Don’t worry, we won’t stop you trying to do your so-called ‘job.’ Do whatever you like if it makes you feel important, it’s a free country. But the big man wants this insurrection stamped on, and stamped on hard. So I’ll give you something to report on. You’ll love this.

Mega Savior. He doesn’t just walk on water. He walks on liberal tears.

You need you some Jesus?.
