The Look

These days, you have to make sure your face fits. He’s very particular about that. We know what he likes and we make sure word gets out to the right people.

The fake news media make fun of it, but then they mock anything we do. They ridicule because they haven’t got a counter-argument, or the right facts, or the sort of integrity we have. They call it ‘Mar-a-Largo Face’. Lip fillers, Botox, chin, cheekbones. Veneers to make that smile really shine. Who hasn’t had work done? All the beautiful people have veneers, and I don’t see you complaining, not unless you can be bothered to look up what that entails.

Don’t think it’s vanity. If the First Lady’s going to avoid the camera, then it’s up to the rest of the ladies to step up and make sure there’s still a bit of glamour. Giving the dad and grandpa voters something to ogle is part of the job.

And it’s a lot of work too. We all get boob jobs, even me. They say a C’s ideal, but others say the bigger, the better. I went for a D, just like the First Daughter. The POTUS likes ‘em big, he likes them to jiggle and bounce. It’ll be a year or so before he’ll be able to get away with saying that in public.

Liposuction from the belly and under the chin. I’m undecided about the BBL. If you go to the wrong place, you’ll get butchered.

It’s not easy. Not many people are willing to be seen in public with me, and that stings. My dad would either die of shame or beat me to death if he was still alive. But then I’m not courting popularity. I’m a true believer, and a true believer knows that some people have to be popular, and some people have to be unpopular.

You know who I am. You’ve done your homework; you know my old name, the one I don’t use now, not in public, anyway. I say it’s my deadname, but that’s only what I say to sound convincing. I make sure to slip up, make it look unconvincing, as if I’m not sure I really meant it. But that name is very much alive, just in hiding, and very much calling the shots. Deep cover, right?

Again, it’s not about popularity, it’s about service. And you’re welcome for my service, by the way, ten years of Hell in the Marine Corps. Of course, when I had the idea and committed to it, I was kicked out, that was the law, His law. I believed in it then and I believe in it now. No major harm done: I self-identified and with a clean record I was honorably discharged. I didn’t rejoin when they changed the law back; no point when they were just going to undo it again!

I saw some doctors, some very expensive doctors, and I told them a cock-and-bull story and paid them enough to go along with it. It’s disgusting, the way they try to pretend to be understanding and non-judgemental. There were interviews, there were questionnaires, they made all the right noises to make it seem like they wanted to take the issue seriously, that I was absolutely certain in my own mind that this is what I needed. They were only too keen to carry out their mutilation, but just wanted to cover their asses and made sure I took my hormones and signed my waivers.

I’d have been happy for them to just operate there and then, my mind was made up, but I had to do it their way, pretend that I was somehow ‘normal’. I just gave them the answers I knew would satisfy them. I’ve always been good at that.

But seriously. Now it’s done. All this? It’s not because I consider this is what I really am. I didn’t ‘realise I was a woman.’ I wasn’t struggling with ‘gender dysphoria’. I maintain publically that is a revolting urge that liberal society wasn’t prepared to check. I am not ‘ill’, I am not ‘struggling’, I am a deviant and it’s time society did something to stop it.

It’s all part of the plan, see. We’ve got Blacks for Trump, Latinos for Trump, Gays for Trump, all reaching out to groups who’d otherwise be inclined to vote against our man. And now there’s Trans for Trump, and that’s me. I don’t think the office could find anyone else convincing. Well, I’ve always been prepared to take one for the team, always have been. Semper Fi. We won’t get the country we want if we’re not prepared to make sacrifices.

Oh, I’m sure the tr-those freaks will be all up in arms, they’ll say I’m acting against my own interests, betraying my own kind, setting the course of trans rights back years. Forcing people back into the closet rather than letting them accept who they are without self-recrimination and judgement, but they miss the point. But they’re not my kind. I’m a man pretending to be a woman, or I’m pretending to be a man pretending to be a woman. It’ll make it more difficult for people to screw with their genders and teach kids that that’s okay and let them screw with their God-given assigned genders and think that’s normal, rather than a filthy perversion. God doesn't make mistakes, so you don't get to correct them. See, what I’m doing isn’t really treachery. How can I betray them, if I was never on their side to begin with?

I don’t feel ashamed of myself, really. Not even of what I’ve done to myself. Oh, yes, it’s disgusting and immoral, but it’s all in a good cause, to make our country great again. So I guess I feel pretty good about that.

In fact, I feel very good, not just about the plan, but in general.

Happy, you know? Within myself.

Comfortable in my own skin.

Sometimes I even smile when I catch myself in the mirror. I’ve got a beautiful smile, everyone would say so. If they didn’t know how unnatural it is. The real beauty, though, is in the sacrifice.
